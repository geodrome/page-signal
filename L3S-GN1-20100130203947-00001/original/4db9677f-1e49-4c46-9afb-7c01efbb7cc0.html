Reading 0-2 Portsmouth
2008-01-01 17:15:00.0
Portsmouth made the best possible start to the new year with a 2-0 win over ten-man Reading at the Madjeski Stadium.
The writing was on the wall for Reading as early as the second minute when Ibrahima Sonko got on the wrong side of Benjani inside the penalty area and denied him a clear goal scoring opportunity, which meant the defender had to be shown a straight red card by Referee Mike Dean.
Niko Krancjar though hit his penalty off the foot of the post leaving Reading somewhat relieved for the short term.
