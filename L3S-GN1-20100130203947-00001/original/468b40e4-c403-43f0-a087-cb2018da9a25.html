FBI to post criminal mug shots on digital billboards

10:02 AM CST on Friday, December 28, 2007

Associated Press

The FBI’s most wanted bank robbers, violent criminals and terrorists will soon appear on 150 digital billboards in 20 cities—including El Paso.

The federal agency has teamed up with Phoenix-based Clear Channel Outdoor to begin airing mug shots.

A successful test run in Philadelphia led to several arrests.

The billboards also will be used to post photos and information on recently committed crimes.
