Driver Arrested After Truck Carrying Asbestos Flips Over In Queens


A driver from Pennsylvania is under arrest after the truck he was driving, which was carrying asbestos, flipped over in Queens yesterday afternoon.

The truck hit a divider just before 2 p.m. on the Clearview Expressway as it was exiting the Long Island Expressway, causing it to overturn. The ramps to the Clearview were closed for more than 12 hours as workers cleaned up the toxic debris.

Police say the driver took off, but he was later arrested. Artur Gaska, 39, faces charges of leaving the scene of an accident.

No one was hurt in the crash. Authorities don't believe the asbestos poses a threat.
