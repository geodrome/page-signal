Nicole Richie and Christina Aguilera give birth Saturday

Nicole Richie and Christina Aguilera both gave birth to healthy babies on Saturday in L.A.

 "Simple Life" star Nicole Richie and singer Christina Aguilera gave birth about an hour apart at the same Los Angeles hospital on Saturday. Christina Aguilera, who married music producer Jordan Bratman in 2005, gave birth to a baby boy, according to reports. Meanwhile Nicole Richie and boyfriend Joel Madden welcomed a baby girl at the Cedars Sinai Medical Center. Both babies are doing fine in perfect condition, as well as the famous moms. According to a statement by a spokesman for Lionel Richie's daughter, Nicole and Madden named their daughter Harlow Winter Kate Madden and have now left the hospital.
