9 aboard Air Canada flight hurt after Airbus hits turbulence

Associated Press - January 10, 2008 3:23 PM ET

CALGARY, Alberta (AP) - Six people are in stable condition after an Air Canada jet made an emergency landing this morning in Calgary, Alberta.

The flight from Victoria, British Columbia, to Toronto was diverted after it hit turbulence, injuring nine people.

The six were taken to a hospital once the plane landed. Three others suffered minor injuries.

One passenger on the plane says her friend was "really hurt" when the turbulence hit, saying she "flew up and hit the ceiling and went back down."

Air Canada says the plane had been carrying 83 passengers and five crew members.
