Romo Gets Ready With Jessica Simpson in Mexico
Dallas’ quarterback Tony Romo got ready for the semifinal against the Giants with his girlfriend in Mexico.
A.E

Dallas Cowboys start Tony Romo got ready for the NFL conference playoff semifinal with his girlfriend Jessica Simpson on the Mexican beaches. This will surely be not well accepted by his team mates who blame Jessica for the quarterback’s bad performances.

The young couple spent five days in the summer resort of Cabo San Lucas with her parents. There are rumours that the present tourist had a very good time.

They left Mexico on Sunday, and there is an important task before Tony and his team mates. The have to beat the New York Giants to get through the conference final and continue the Super Bowl.
