22yo 'ran over and killed' girlfriend

Posted January 2, 2008 08:38:00

  • Map: Canberra 2600

ACT police have charged a Sydney man with manslaughter after he allegedly ran over his girlfriend at Gungahlin in Canberra's north yesterday.

Police say the 22-year-old man from Campbelltown in Sydney's south-west was visiting relatives and friends with his 18-year-old girlfriend for New Year's Eve celebrations.

They say witnesses have told them the couple had an argument just before the man ran over the woman.

She suffered serious injuries and died later in hospital.

The 22-year-old man will appear in court later today.
