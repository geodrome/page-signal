Friday December 28 2007

ACTRESS Mischa Barton is now following down the well-worn jail path of Hollywood friends Paris Hilton, Nicole Richie and Lindsay Lohan.

The star, best known for her portrayal as the spoilt Marissa Cooper on The OC, was briefly jailed on Thursday on suspicion of drunken driving, marijuana possession and driving without a licence, Los Angeles police have said.

Her arrest comes two weeks since the starlet made an appearance on the Late Late Show.

She was accompanied by her Irish mother Nuala Quinn and was in Ireland for the world premiere of Closing The Ring in Belfast.

LA police revealed that Barton was briefly jailed before posting $10,000 (€6,800) bail following her arrest in the early hours of Thursday.

The 21-year-old was pulled over by traffic police while driving over in West Hollywood around 3am on Thursday morning.

Police reports said her vehicle was straddling two lanes of traffic and said she failed to signal when making a turn.

"During Ms Barton's detention, it was determined that she was an unlicensed driver and was driving while under the influence of an alcoholic beverage," the official report said.

She was also suspected to be in possession of marijuana and an unidentified controlled substance, said Sgt John Hocking.

The arrest comes at the end of a troublesome year for Barton which has seen three of her friends arrested and jailed, albeit briefly, for similar offences.

The English-born actress made her movie debut in the 1997 Sundance Film Festival favorite Lawn Dogs and appeared in such films as The Sixth Sense and Notting Hill.

Although well-known to many in the industry, it wasn't until she landed the role of Marissa Cooper on The OC, that she was propelled to worldwide attention.

However, Barton opted to quit the show after just three season. The show was cancelled soon afterwards due to plummeting ratings.

Barton's arrest comes just as her film career is at a critical point.

Her role in romantic wartime epic, Closing The Ring, had recieved some critical acclaim before the incident. The film also stars Richard Attenborough and Shirley MacLaine.

She attended the world premiere in Belfast before flying to Dublin to chat with Pat Kenny. She was later seen hitting the tiles in Krystle Nightclub on Harcourt Street.
