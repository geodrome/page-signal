Om Shanti Om    
Director: Farah Khan
Cast:Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padhukone, Arjun Rampal, Shreyas Talpade

The movie is about a junior artist who has ambitions to become a megastar. He wants to lead a luxurious life. Om Prakash Makhija, the character played by Shahrukh Khan secretly loves a film actress called Shanti Priya played by Deepika Padukone.

Only a couple of people including his mother have faith in Makhija’s talent. A fire accident on the sets leads to the chance meeting between Shanti and Om. Om falls in love with Shanti and feels fulfilled in her company. But later he discovers that Shanti is already married.

Shanti’s husband is a film producer named Mukesh Mehra played by Arjun Rampal. Mehra kills his wife after she gets pregnant. Since Om is a witness to this crime, he too gets murdered by Mehra’s gang.

Om is reincarnated as Om Kapoor who is a star-kid. Kapoor conspires with Sandy who is Shanti’s clone to kill Mehra. Mehra is by this time a famous Hollywood producer. Om’s objective is to provoke Mehra to confess his crime.

This movie is a major disappointment. It is hilarious when the director tries to infuse sentimental scenes. Not even a one-time watch.
