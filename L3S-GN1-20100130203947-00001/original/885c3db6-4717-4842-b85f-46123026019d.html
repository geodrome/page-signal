More agony for Robinson

Jan 5, 2008

Tottenham and Reading served up another thriller in a 2-2 draw at White Hart Lane but it was a miserable afternoon for England goalkeeper Paul Robinson.

Robinson failed to deal with Stephen Hunt's free-kick for the opener in the FA Cup third-round clash, then he failed to hold onto a Leroy Lita shot for Hunt's equaliser.

Dimitar Berbatov had scored twice between the errors, then Tom Huddlestone was dismissed late on as the sides provided more entertainment following their 6-4 league clash the previous weekend.
