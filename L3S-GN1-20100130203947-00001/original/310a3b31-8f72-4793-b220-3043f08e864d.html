Aidy recalls big guns
By Kevin Affleck
Read Comments (1)

DAN SHITTU and Marlon King have been recalled to the starting line-up for this afternoon's Championship game between Southampton and Watford at St Mary's.

Shittu, who has not started a match since the 3-2 win at Colchester, replaces Matt Jackson, who drops out of the squad altogether, while King gets the nod ahead of Nathan Ellington to partner Darius Henderson in attack.

Southampton: Davis; Thomas, Davies, Bennett, Wright, Viafara, Surman, Safri (c), Euell, Wright-Phillips, John.

Watford: Lee; Doyley, Shittu, DeMerit, Stewart; Smith, Williamson, Francis, McAnuff; King, Henderson.

Subs: Poom, Ellington, Mariappa, Ainsowrth, O'Toole.
