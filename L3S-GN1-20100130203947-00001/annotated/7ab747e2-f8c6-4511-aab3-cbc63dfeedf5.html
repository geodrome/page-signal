DiCaprio attacker?s extradition sought

A woman who fled to Canada after slashing Leonardo DiCaprio with a broken beer bottle at a party in 2005 could be extradited back to the US.

Aretha Wilson has been in police custody for several months in Toronto in connection with an aggravated assault case there, and is also a suspect in other assault cases.

DiCaprio suffered "great bodily injury" when Wilson allegedly attacked him with a broken beer bottle in June 2005, as he was leaving a party in Hollywood Hills. An arrest warrant has charged her with one count of assault with a deadly weapon.



[ Published: 8-Jan-2008 ]
