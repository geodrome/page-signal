Lecavalier and Iginla named All-Star captains

New York, NY (Sports Network) - Tampa Bay Lightning center Vincent Lecavalier and Calgary Flames right wing Jarome Iginla have been selected as captains for their respective squads at the NHL All-Star Game.

Lecavalier, who entered Thursday's action tied for the NHL lead with 63 points, will captain the East. Iginla, just one point off the pace, will captain the West.

Both players were voted as starters in fan balloting.

The All-Star Game is scheduled for Sunday, January 27 in Atlanta.
