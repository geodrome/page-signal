NATO Intervention in Pakistan: Dion

Josh Pringle

Wednesday, January 16, 2008

Liberal leader Stephane Dion suggests a "diplomatic solution" is needed by NATO in Pakistan.

Fresh off his trip to Afghanistan, Dion told reporters NATO intervention could be necessary in Pakistan as a way to bring peace to neighbouring Afghanistan.

Dion says NATO may have to look at other options to flush out Taliban militants who are taking refuge in Pakistan, saying if Pakistan leaders are not able to do it on their own "it is something we could consider with NATO, how to help Pakistan bring peace to Afghanistan."

The Liberals stress any solution would not be a military one.
