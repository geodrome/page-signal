Soccer

Chelsea hoping to sign Anelka before weekend

  • Email Article
  • Print Version
  • Search
  • Most Emailed
  • Most Read


Wednesday, January 09, 2008

Chelsea are hoping to complete the signing of Nicolas Anelka in time for this weekend's Premier League clash with Tottenham.

Bolton have already rejected a Chelsea bid for the Frenchman, but are apparently willing to let him go for a fee of around ?15m.

Chelsea are hoping to finalise negotiations as soon as possible as their three main strikers are currently unavailable due to injury and the African Nations Cup.
