Rocket down Eagles

Geoff Walker and Lucas McKinley each scored in the final five minutes of regulation as the Prince Edward Island Rocket defeated the host Cape Breton Screaming Eagles 4-3 in Quebec Major Junior Hockey League action last night before 3,502 fans.



Dean Ouellet, with two, and Robert Slaney scored for Cape Breton.



At Saint John, Sydney's Ryan Sparling scored twice as the Sea Dogs knocked off the Lewiston Maineiacs 6-4. With the win, Saint John is tied for first place in the East Division with Cape Breton. The idle Halifax Mooseheads are a point back. - Staff
