Terry set for scan

Jan 13 2008

John Terry will have a scan on his foot injury on Monday.

The Chelsea captain has been out of action since breaking three bones against Arsenal on December 16 - and said on Saturday the deadline of being fit to play for England against Switzerland, Fabio Capello's first match in charge, next month is a "small target".

The defender reiterated that prognosis on Sky Sports today, adding he is likely to have a clearer picture after another check on how his injury is healing.

When asked when he expects to be playing again on Sky Sports on Sunday, Terry said: "I am hoping two or three weeks. I have my scan tomorrow (Monday) morning."
