Iraqi soldier fires on Americans

Updated 22.16 Sat Jan 05 2008
Keywords: Mosul , Iraq

An Iraqi soldier has killed two US troops on a joint patrol in the northern city of Mosul.

The gunman also wounded three others along with a civilian interpreter after opening fire, Iraqi and American officials said.

The dead soldiers were named as Captain Rowdy Inman and Sergeant Benjamin Portell, both of the 3rd Armored Cavalry Regiment

The dead soldiers were named as Captain Rowdy Inman and Sergeant Benjamin Portell, both of the 3rd Armored Cavalry Regiment.

The attacker reportedly had links to Sunni Arab insurgent groups.

A spokesman for the US military said: "The Iraqi soldier who allegedly opened fire fled the scene but was identified by other Iraqi army personnel and was then apprehended.

"Two Iraqi army soldiers are now being held in connection with the incident."
