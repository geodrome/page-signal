Hope update: Gamez takes 3rd-round lead

04:52 PM PST on Friday, January 18, 2008

By MIRJAM SWANSON

THE PRESS-ENTERPRISE

PALM DESERT ? When the dust settled on the third round Friday at the 49 th Bob Hope Chrysler Classic, it was Robert Gamez?s name all alone on top of the leaderboard.

 Despite a couple of bogeys, Gamez carded a 5-under-par 67 at PGA West to take a 1-shot lead with two rounds left.

 Justin Leonard and D.J. Trahan are tied for second. Boo Weekley launched himself into contention at 2 strokes back with a 10-under 62 at PGA West.

 Hometown favorite Anthony Kim ? formerly a star at La Quinta High ? also surged further into contention with a 67 at SilverRock Resort. He?s among four players tied for fifth, 4 shots off the pace.

 ?I?ve been making my par putts, but not enough birdie putts,? said Kim, who missed three putts from within 5 feet. ?In the next few days, I?m going to try to make a couple more birdies.?

 Former Palm Desert High player Jason Allred shot 70 and is tied for 101 st with Saturday?s cut on the horizon.

 The conditions cooperated for a third straight day, as limited wind and warming temperatures helped scoring.
