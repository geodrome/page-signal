Hester Runs Bears to Win in Season Finale Save Email Print
Posted: 6:12 PM Dec 30, 2007

Last Updated: 6:10 PM Dec 31, 2007

0 comments
A | A | A

The New Orleans Saints failed to extend their season, losing to the Chicago Bears 33-25.

Devin Hester helped the Bears win a rematch of last season's NFC title game. He returned a punt 64 yards for his sixth runback for a score this year, breaking the record he set last year. Hester also caught a 55-yard touchdown pass from Kyle Orton, who was 12-of-27 for 190 yards and two TDs.

Adrian Peterson ran for 91 yards and threw an option pass to Bernard Berrian for a 9-yard touchdown in the second quarter.

Drew Brees threw 60 times as he tried to keep the Saints' postseason hopes alive. Brees completed 35 for 320 yards, three touchdowns and two interceptions.

Both teams finish 7-and-9.
